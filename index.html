Foster Finder
GitHub

A Flask application to help stray animals find foster homes.

Foster Finder is a Flask application designed to help veterinary clinics and animal shelters find willing foster homes, while also reducing the call frequency for individuals willing to foster. The application creates a shared list of volunteers between clinics and shelters and cycles volunteers to the end of the list once they have been contacted. The more volunteers added to the pool, the lower the frequency every individual will be contacted.
